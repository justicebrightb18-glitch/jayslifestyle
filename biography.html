Jays Biography


UCHENDU JUSTICE BRIGHT, popularly known as JayJay, is a 26-year-old communication professional from Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. He began his educational journey with his early schooling, which he completed in 2008, before proceeding to Nazareth International School for his secondary education. There, he sat for the National Examinations Council (NECO) in 2015 and passed with outstanding results.

Driven by a passion for media and storytelling, Justice pursued tertiary education in Mass Communication. He gained admission into Kwara State University in 2021, where he excelled academically and graduated at the top of his class in December 2025, earning a Bachelor of Science degree in Mass Communication.

Justice is presently a practicing journalist, known for his commitment to accuracy, creativity, and impactful communication. Alongside journalism, he has also built strong competence in Public Relations and Advertising, demonstrating versatility across multiple branches of the communication field.

Focused, ambitious, and dedicated to professional growth, Justice continues to build his career with the goal of making meaningful contributions to the media landscape in Nigeria and beyond.
